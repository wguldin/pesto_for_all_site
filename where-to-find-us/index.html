Now availablein stores!

After three amazing years at the Farmers Market of the Ozarks, our basil pesto is now also available at several retailers throughout Springfield!

Resealable freezer pouches of our pesto, which we've molded into small pieces, are available at Brown Derby Wine Center. And soon, we'll also be on shelves at Mama Jean's Natural Market!

Follow us for our latest updates

Brown Derby Wine Center

After making our fresh pesto, we immediately freeze it in small molds that are easy to use in all your favorite recipes. These pesto "half moon" pieces are then packaged in a reasealable pouch, and stay fresh for at least 3 months.

As you walk into Brown Derby, you can find our pesto in the freezer on your right.

Mama Jean's Natural Market

Our frozen pesto will be available at all three locations of Mama Jean's Natural Market in the next few weeks. You'll find it in the freezer aisle of each store, usually near other frozen pasta options.

Follow us on Instagram to find out when we'll be there officially!

Farmers Market of the Ozarks

Although we're in grocery stores now, we still love selling our fresh pesto at Farmers Market of the Ozarks!

We're there pretty much every Saturday from 8 a.m. to 1 p.m., from Late May until Early October. Follow us on Instagram to see when we'll be there!

The freshest pesto you can buy

We stone grind fresh basil grown on our farm with unfiltered olive oil, sea salt and garlic to create a wonderfully tasty pesto. Oh, did we tell you it's nut and dairy free too?

Get some pesto!

Our farm

All of our pesto is made from basil grown on our farm outside Mountain Grove, Missouri. We harvest our basil leaves at peak flavor, and we grind them onsite within a few hours.

Our inspiration

Our honeymoon in Italy was the first time we tried fresh pesto. And we couldn't believe how much better it was than the stuff in stores! Once we got back, we started making our own pesto and haven't stopped since.

About Megan & Will

After developing our recipe, we co-founded Pesto For All two years ago. You can find us on most summer weekends at the Farmers Market of the Ozarks, and we're hoping to be in local grocery stores this year.
